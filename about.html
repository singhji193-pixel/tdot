At T-DOT Towing, we understand that vehicle breakdowns are stressful and inconvenient. That's why we've built our reputation on two fundamental principles: lightning-fast response times and exceptional customer service.

Operating throughout Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area, our team of licensed professionals is available 24/7 to assist you with any roadside emergency. With state-of-the-art equipment and years of experience, we handle everything from luxury vehicle transport to heavy-duty commercial towing with the utmost care and professionalism.
